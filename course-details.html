Linux Pro Administration

Certification Training

This very high technical level training aims to give you the knowledge and skills necessary to administer a GNU/Linux system whatever the distribution. It gives you skills in terms of server infrastructure administration, network administration and the basic services associated with it.

This LPIC-1 ( Professional Linux Administrator ) certification program from the Linux Professional Institute is known to recruiters as being an official recognition of qualification, competence and ability to occupy an Administrator position.

Training aims

You will learn, among other things, how to install and configure server infrastructures, and to guarantee the availability and sustainability of the information stored in these servers.

At the end of the training, the participant will be able to:

  • At the end of the training, the participant will be able to:
  • Manage common disks, partitions and file systems
  • Install and configure a GNU/Linux system on a PC and Server type station
  • Use the system's systemd runlevels and targets
  • Install and uninstall programs on distributions of the RedHat and Debian families
  • Perform backups and updates

Teaching Methods

Our trainers are true expert consultants, with solid theoretical engineering training, supplemented by many years of experience and professional certifications. Our educational tools are adapted and chosen according to the educational project.

This training is composed of:

  • Courses supplemented by Demonstrations
  • Exercises and Practical Work;
  • Course materials;
  • Laboratories;
  • Preparation kits (mock exams).

At the end of the training, each participant automatically benefits from 20 days of coaching intended to provide follow-up for students wishing to take the LPI101 and LPI102 exams .
