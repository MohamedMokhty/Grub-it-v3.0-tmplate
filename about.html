About Grub-IT

We Offer Quality Training to Our Clients

Grub-IT is a global player in IT training based in Cameroon, and present in France and the United States via partnerships.

A multi-specialist, Grub-IT covers several leading areas of IT to meet the challenges of its clients Infrastructure, Systems, Networks, Development, Cloud computing, Security, Cybersecurity, etc.

Rich in strong values, Grub-IT is a Professional Training Center for IT professions, recognized for the quality of support for its employees and clients (large accounts, higher education institutes, universities, SMEs, students and individuals).
